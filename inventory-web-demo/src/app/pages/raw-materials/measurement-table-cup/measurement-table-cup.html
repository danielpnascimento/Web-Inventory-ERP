Standardization of Measures (Important)
Register everything in grams (g) or ml.
(KG and L removed to ensure production calculation accuracy).

Golden Rule: Divide or multiply by 1,000 to convert.

For water: 1g = 1ml. For others (ex: milk/oil), density may vary slightly.
