Tips for Converting Recipes
Register everything in grams (g) or ml. Standard: 1 Cup = 240ml.
(Removed KG and L to ensure the accuracy of production calculations).
Standard Dry Goods (1 Cup)
  • All-Purpose Flour: 120g a 130g
  • Granulated Sugar: 180g a 200g
  • Cocoa / Chocolate Powder: 90g a 100g
  • Raw Rice: 200g
  • Rolled Oats: 80g
Liquids & Pastes (1 Cup)
  • Water, Milk, Oil: 240ml to 250ml (or g)
  • Butter / Margarine: 200g to 230g
Fractions (Reference for Flour)
  • 1/2 Cup: ~60g | 1/3 Cup: ~40g
  • 1/4 Cup: ~30g | 1 Tablespoon: ~15g
Tablespoon (Standard 15ml)
  • Liquids / Salt: 15g
  • Sugar: 12g | Butter: 14g
  • Flour: 7,5g a 10g
  • Cocoa: 6g a 10g
Reference for Ferments (1 Tablespoon)
Chemical (Powder)
~14g
Biological Dry
~10g
